What is Table Tennis?

Table tennis, also known as ping-pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball, also known as a ping-pong ball, back and forth across a table using small solid rackets.

The game takes place on a hard table divided by a net. Players use a variety of spins and angles to outmaneuver their opponents, making it a fast-paced and exciting sport!
